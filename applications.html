Application Services

Shift your IT spend from legacy to Cutting edge. Modern applications deliver the experience customers want and the results you need. Blue9 Technologies can help you accelerate to a secure, digital, data-driven enterprise.

Services and Solutions

We use advanced technologies and methods to accelerate the creation, modernization, upgradation and delivery and maintenance of high-quality, secure applications allowing you to to re-engineer innovate your business faster while reducing risk, time to market, and total cost of ownership.​ Our Application Services cover four areas.

Modern Application Development

Our services accelerate your transition to a secure, digital, data-driven enterprise through the rapid development of cloud-native and custom applications, along with microservices/APIs. We incorporate proprietary AI, automation, and machine learning models, while applying Agile and DevOps principles and integrat it to your technology environment. Blue9 has the capability and scale to deliver secure applications across various cloud platforms, including private cloud, with speed and quality using modern delivery methods and DevOps to enrich user experience, accelerate business outcomes. Our Modern Application Development services span the entire cloud or custom application lifecycle aligned to today’s dynamic business environment.


Testing and Digital Assurance

Blue9's end-to-end testing and digital assurance services enable clients to release secure, quality applications quickly and cost-effectively, ensuring your customers enjoy a great experience from the first time they use the application. We harness the latest test automation capabilities, capitalizing on ‘shift left’, agile, and DevSecOps opportunities to maximize customer speed to value with seamless technology transformation. Blue9’s can also provide Application Support services to ensure your application works as it has been designed to work. The support services could be for the newly installed Applications by us or for your existing application stack. However the support to your existing application stack would provided only after proper examination and a due deligence alongwith a proper KT and handing over of the application stack.


Application Modernization

Unlock the business value in your legacy applications and accelerate your move to multi-cloud using industry-leading tools and agile best practices to upgrade, migrate and modernize your applications. Blue9 can transform applications management landscapes by deploying next generation lean, analytics and automation tools. Our capabilities mean we can integrate and operate diverse application landscapes ranging from modern cloud applications to traditional (legacy) applications. We deliver best in class productivity and service standards enabling customers to remain focused on their digital transformation strategies.​​

Partnering for business transformation
