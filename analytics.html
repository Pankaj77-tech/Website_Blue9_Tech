Information Governance

Most enterprises in the current age of information era handle vast amounts of physical and electronic data, often with insecure or expensive storage media, informal retention procedures and minimum regard to the prevalent retention, disposition and data privacy regulations. Without a solid governance and protection plan that ensures data security and effective management of information assets, valuable business data can be slow to retrieve, expensive to store, and at risk of security and privacy breaches. Blue9's Information Governance solution is designed to help you formulate, implement, and optimize a data management and storage strategy that views IT, business objectives, and external requirements collectively. Whether you have no system in place or aging legacy systems, Blue9 will align with your current state and help scale to your desired level of maturity. With flexible options, you will be able to classify, archive and manage physical and electronic data reliably and cost-effectively.
